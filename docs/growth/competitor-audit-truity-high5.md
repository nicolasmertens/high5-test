# Competitor Audit — Truity & HIGH5 Test

**Date:** 2026-04-16
**Auditor:** Head of Growth Agent, 1Test

---

## Truity (truity.com)

### Overview
Largest personality test platform in the US. 50M+ test takers. Featured in Bloomberg, CNBC, Inc., Business Insider. Founded as Truity Psychometrics.

### Product
- 7 tests: Enneagram, TypeFinder (16 Personalities), Big Five, DISC, Career Aptitude, Love Styles, Emotional Intelligence
- Free tier: summary results only
- Paid: $12-29 for full reports
- B2B product: Truity at Work (team assessments, purchased credits)

### Content Strategy
- Active blog with pop-culture hooks (Bridgerton, Valentine's Day, AI)
- Uses trademarked names freely (Myers-Briggs, MBTI)
- 50+ blog posts with strong SEO presence
- Personality type library with dedicated pages per type
- Career search tool by personality type

### Pricing
- Free: basic results (teaser)
- Individual full report: ~$12-29
- Team pricing: credit-based

### Strengths (their advantages)
- Domain authority from 50M+ users and press mentions
- Multiple test types including Big Five (which we don't offer)
- B2B revenue stream with team analytics
- Career search tool — unique differentiator
- Uses trademarked names (gives them SEO juice from "MBTI" searches)

### Weaknesses (our opportunities)
- **Paywall on full results** — our biggest differentiator. Truity shows a teaser then charges.
- **Uses trademarked names** — this is a legal risk for them but an SEO advantage. We cannot replicate.
- **Fragmented experience** — each test is separate. 1Test offers all four in one platform.
- **No multi-framework comparison** — users take one test and get one perspective. 1Test shows all four together.
- **Blog relies on pop culture** — less practical, more entertainment. Our blog is action-oriented.

### SEO Threat Level: HIGH
Truity ranks for virtually every personality test keyword. They are the primary competitor to beat in organic search.

## HIGH5 Test (high5test.com)

### Overview
Strengths-only platform. 4M+ users. Positions as the free alternative to CliftonStrengths. B2B focused with team analytics.

### Product
- One test: HIGH5 Strengths Assessment (120 questions, ~20 min)
- Free tier: top 5 strengths only
- Paid: Full Strengths Report ($?), Career Report, Team Report, Student Report
- B2B platform: team analytics, 360 feedback, interpersonal intelligence, strengths academy

### Content Strategy
- Blog covering personal development, career, leadership, interviews, team building
- Comparison pages (CliftonStrengths vs HIGH5, Kolbe vs HIGH5, VIA vs HIGH5)
- FAQ-heavy product pages (good for long-tail SEO)
- Multi-language support (Dutch, Italian, Polish, Hindi, Filipino)

### Pricing
- Free: top 5 strengths only
- Paid: full report, career report, team report (prices not publicly listed — must register)
- Coach certification program
- B2B: demo-based sales

### Strengths (their advantages)
- Strong B2B product with team analytics, 360 feedback, weekly insights
- Coach certification ecosystem — creates a distribution channel
- Comparison pages capture "HIGH5 vs CliftonStrengths" intent
- Fortune 500 social proof on homepage
- Multi-language support

### Weaknesses (our opportunities)
- **One framework only** — no DISC, no Enneagram, no 16 Personalities
- **Paywall on detailed results** — same freemium trap as Truity
- **Strengths-only positioning** — misses users searching for DISC or Enneagram
- **No blog authority** — less content breadth than Truity or 1Test
- **Opaque pricing** — users must register to see prices

### SEO Threat Level: MEDIUM
HIGH5 ranks for "free strengths test" but doesn't compete on DISC, Enneagram, or 16 Personalities keywords.

## Competitive Positioning for 1Test

### Our Unique Advantages

1. **No paywall, ever.** Complete results across four frameworks. Both Truity and HIGH5 gate detailed results behind payment.
2. **Multi-framework integration.** Users see DISC + Enneagram + 16 Personalities + Strengths together. No competitor does this.
3. **Content volume.** 46 content pages live, targeting 43+ keyword clusters. Rapidly expanding.
4. **No trademarked names.** Legal safety but SEO disadvantage on "MBTI" queries.

### Our Gaps vs Competitors

1. **No Big Five test.** Truity offers this. Consider adding if demand exists.
2. **No career search tool.** Truity has a career-by-type search. High intent keyword opportunity.
3. **No B2B team product.** Both Truity and HIGH5 have team analytics. Our viral loop (TESA-80) addresses this partially.
4. **No press mentions or domain authority.** Both competitors have years of backlinks. We need link-building.
5. **No comparison pages.** HIGH5 captures "X vs Y" keywords. We should create comparison landing pages.
6. **No multi-language.** HIGH5 has 5 languages. Consider for future.

### Recommended Actions

1. **Create comparison pages:** "DISC vs Enneagram", "16 Personalities vs DISC", "Free personality test comparison" — capture bottom-of-funnel search intent
2. **Build a career matching tool:** Even a simple "careers by type" page would capture high-intent job-related searches
3. **Pursue press mentions:** Reach out to career bloggers, productivity writers, HR publications
4. **Link-building outreach:** Our "free vs paid personality tests" article is a natural link magnet
5. **Monitor Truity's blog topics:** Their pop-culture hooks (Bridgerton, AI) generate social shares — we should watch but not copy their approach
6. **Add FAQ-rich pages for each framework:** HIGH5's FAQ-heavy product pages rank well for long-tail queries
