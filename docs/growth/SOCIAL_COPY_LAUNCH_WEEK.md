## Usage Notes

- **LinkedIn**: Post one per day, max 2 per day. Space 4-6 hours apart if posting twice.
- **Twitter/X**: Space posts 2-3 hours apart. Thread formats (Tweets 4, 6) get more engagement than single tweets.
- **Reddit**: Post in r/personality, r/psychology, r/selfimprovement, r/careerguidance. Check subreddit rules before posting — some disallow self-promotion on Monday-Friday.
- **Angle selection**: Founder story and data point angles perform best on LinkedIn. Question and list formats perform best on Twitter.
- **Attribution**: All social copy is from "1Test" or should be posted by the Founder under their personal account for authenticity.
