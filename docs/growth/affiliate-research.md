# 1Test Affiliate Program Research

> Internal research document for personality test result page monetization.
> **Note:** Do NOT use trademarked names (MBTI, Myers-Briggs, CliftonStrengths, StrengthsFinder, DiSC with lowercase i).
> Use: "16 Personalities", "DISC", "Strengths", "Enneagram".

**Date:** April 2026
**Assumptions:** 2-3% click-through rate on recommendations, ~3% conversion rate on landing pages (industry avg for warm traffic), 1000 unique visitors to a result page.

---

## Executive Summary

1Test result pages are high-intent, personality-specific moments where users are primed to learn more about themselves. Each assessment type (DISC, Enneagram, 16 Personalities, Strengths) creates a natural context for recommending books, courses, career tools, and coaching. The key strategy is **matching the affiliate product to the personality result** — e.g., career services on DISC results, relationship books on Enneagram results, leadership courses on Strengths results.

**Estimated total revenue per 1000 visitors across all categories:** $80-$350 depending on placement, relevance, and traffic quality.

## 6. Recommended Implementation Strategy

### Phase 1: Quick Win (Week 1-2)

7. **LinkedIn Learning** — Professional development complement for career-focused assessments.
8. **Headspace/Calm** — Wellness upsell for Enneagram and introverted 16 Personality types.

## 7. Legal & Compliance Notes

- **FTC Disclosure:** All affiliate links must include clear disclosure. Place a visible disclaimer on result pages: "Some links on this page may earn us a commission at no cost to you."
- **Trademark Compliance:** Never use protected trademarks (MBTI, Myers-Briggs, CliftonStrengths, StrengthsFinder, DiSC with lowercase i) in any affiliate marketing copy, ad text, or link URLs.
- **Approved Terminology:** Use "16 Personalities" (not MBTI), "DISC" (not DiSC), "Strengths" (not CliftonStrengths/StrengthsFinder).
- **Amazon Associates Specific:** Must identify as an Amazon Associate; cannot use Amazon trademarks in domain names; cannot send affiliate links in email as primary channel; must maintain 3 qualifying sales per 180 days.
- **Cookie Consent:** Ensure GDPR/CCPA compliance for cookie tracking on result pages.
- **No Pay-Per-Click:** Do not bid on trademarked terms (e.g., "Enneagram test") to drive traffic to affiliate pages unless you own the brand.
