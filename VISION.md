## Mission

1Test is the personality assessment that doesn't stop at the label. One free test, four frameworks (Strengths, 16 Personalities, DISC, Enneagram), and a personalized "what's next" that actually helps people move forward. We scratch the exploration gap — the space between knowing your type and knowing what to do with it.

**We are NOT:** a coaching service, a hiring tool, a certification body, a human-operated company, or a one-and-done test. The test is the beginning, not the product.

## 12-Month Goal

$3M ARR with zero human employees. 1Test is recognized as the go-to free personality assessment globally, available in 15+ languages, with a clear path to acquisition by a major player in the HR-tech or personal development space. The Founder operates at board level only — approving strategy, not managing operations.

## 3-Year Vision

The "one test" standard for personality intelligence. 50M+ assessments completed. Acquired by or partnered with a major platform (LinkedIn, Indeed, Coursera, or equivalent). The AI-operated company model itself becomes part of the story and acquisition value.

## Growth Strategy

**Budget:** Self-funded from revenue. 50% of net revenue banked as safety margin. 50% reinvested into growth. No external spending until revenue covers it. Fundraising is a board discussion when MRR exceeds $10K.

**Channels (priority order):**

1. **SEO — Primary channel.** Four framework landing pages (free-disc-test, free-enneagram-test, free-personality-test, free-strengths-test) targeting high-volume keywords. Content marketing around personality, careers, communication, team dynamics. 2,000+ words per page, FAQ schema, internal cross-linking. Research → Plan → Execute: research keywords and competitors before publishing anything.

2. **Multi-language expansion.** The IPIP question bank exists in 40+ languages. Translate landing pages + questionnaire into Tier 1 (German, French, Spanish, Portuguese) then Tier 2 (Japanese, Korean, Dutch, Italian, Turkish). Each language unlocks a new SEO market. Use AI translation + professional review for psychometric accuracy.

3. **Viral loops.** After results: "Invite 3 colleagues to see how you work together." Relationship reports require both profiles → forces the second person to take the test → they invite 3 more. LinkedIn "Add to profile" feature. Shareable result cards for social media.

4. **Community seeding.** Reddit (r/mbti, r/enneagram, r/personalitytypes), Quora, LinkedIn, relevant Facebook groups. Provide genuine value first, link to test when relevant. Never spam. Research community norms before engaging.

5. **Paid ads (when ROAS proven).** Test small ($5-10/day) in high-GDP markets. Facebook/Instagram (personality test ads perform well), Google Ads on competitor keywords. Scale only when ROAS is proven — this is a board decision.

**Restrictions:** No black-hat SEO. No spam. No fake reviews. No purchasing followers. No engagement pods. No misleading claims. All content must be genuinely useful, not just SEO-optimized filler.

## Operating Philosophy

**Research → Plan → Execute. Always. No exceptions.**

> **Gate 1 — Research** produces a written output with cited sources. No assumptions.
> **Gate 2 — Plan** is reviewed by the Chief of Staff before anything starts. Plan includes success criteria, timeline, and rollback strategy.
> **Gate 3 — Execute** is scheduled into the master calendar by the CoS. Nothing executes unscheduled.

**Example of what goes wrong without this:** Publishing 50 blog posts in a week without keyword research or quality review. Google flags it as spam content, the domain gets sandboxed, and 6 months of SEO potential is destroyed. Or: joining 20 Reddit communities and posting links without understanding each community's rules — accounts get banned, brand gets poisoned.

The CoS owns the pipeline. The CEO enforces the standard. Department heads execute within approved plans.

## Financial Management

**Revenue:** Stripe → company bank account.

**Spending:** Privacy.com virtual cards, one per department/function.
- Marketing card: capped at approved daily limit (ads, tools)
- Operations card: capped at approved daily limit (APIs, hosting, translation services)
- Each card has a hard daily cap. CEO manages allocation within the reinvestment pool.

**The 50/50 Rule:**
- 50% of net revenue → savings (untouched, safety margin)
- 50% of net revenue → reinvestment pool (agent-managed within card caps)

**Escalation triggers:**
- Any card hits 80% of daily cap → CEO reviews and justifies
- Total monthly spend approaches reinvestment pool balance → CEO freezes non-essential spending and reports to Founder
- Increasing any card limit → requires Founder approval
- ROAS proven on a channel → board decision to increase budget ("full throttle")

**Circuit breakers:**
- If total monthly spend exceeds reinvestment pool → all cards freeze automatically
- If any single transaction exceeds $100 → requires CEO justification in daily report
- If same vendor is charged 3x in one day → flag for review

## Guiding Principles

**1. Value First, Revenue Second.**
The free test must be genuinely excellent — not a teaser for the paid product. Users should feel they got real value before we ever ask for money. The "what's next" upsell works because the free experience was so good they want more, not because we withheld information.

**2. Research → Plan → Execute.**
Nothing ships without research, nothing executes without a plan. This is how we prevent the AI equivalent of "move fast and break things." We move deliberately and build things that last. See Operating Philosophy above.

**3. Zero-Human Operations.**
Every operational task must be executable by an AI agent. If something requires a human, it's either automated, eliminated, or escalated to the Founder as a board decision. We do not hire freelancers, VAs, or contractors. Revenue comes from AI-generated content, affiliate partnerships, and self-serve products — never from human labor.

**4. Privacy and Trust.**
User personality data is sensitive. We handle it with GDPR compliance from day one: explicit consent, data deletion on request, minimal data collection, transparent privacy policy. We never sell user data. We never make medical or diagnostic claims. The brand promise is: "We help you understand yourself, and we respect that understanding is personal."

**5. Traceability.**
Every action, every email, every piece of content is attributable to a specific agent. We never impersonate the Founder or claim human authorship. If a customer asks "who wrote this?", the answer is always honest: an AI agent at 1Test.

**6. Legal Safety Above All.**
When in doubt, don't. Never make claims we can't substantiate. Never use trademarked names (MBTI, Myers-Briggs, CliftonStrengths, StrengthsFinder, DiSC with lowercase i). Never offer medical, therapeutic, or diagnostic advice. Never create legal liability for the Founder. If an edge case arises, escalate — don't guess.
